```
+-------------------+        +------------------+        +-------------------+

|   Assessment      |        |   Execution      |        | Validation        |

+-------------------+     +-------------------+      +-------------------+
```

### Explanation of Each Stage

1. **Pre-Migration Assessment**
   - **Inventory & Compatibility Check**: Assess current hardware and software to ensure compatibility with the new OS.
   - **Resource Allocation**: Plan resources needed for the migration, including personnel, time, and financial budgets.

2. **Migration Execution**
   - **Data Transfer & Backup**: Ensure all critical data is backed up and transferred safely.
   - **OS Installation & Configuration**: Install the new operating system and configure it according to the organization's needs.

3. **Post-Migration Validation**
   - **Testing & Troubleshooting**: Conduct thorough testing to ensure all systems work as expected. Resolve any issues that arise.
   - **Performance Monitoring**: Continuously monitor the system's performance to ensure stability and efficiency.

Each stage involves careful planning and execution to minimize downtime and data loss. The diagram outlines a typical flow, but specific processes can vary based on the organization's needs and the complexity of the migration.
